About Me

I am a simple girl. I have a busy life and a full schedule. When I have time to read, I want to read something meaningful, something that touches my soul, something that takes me to worlds I will otherwise never see, something I will feel only in the books, adventures I will take upon only on pages.
If I am reading your book, I gave you my money and my attention. Don't Waste My Time.
